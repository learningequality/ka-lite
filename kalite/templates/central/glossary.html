Glossary

  1. Facility

    A facility is the place where the server is located that enables sharing within a zone.

  2. Organization

    An organization is an entity responsible for administering multiple different zones and facilities.

  3. Zone

    A zone is the area in which multiple devices may connect to share content with each other.
