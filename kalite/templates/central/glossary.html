Glossary

  • Facility
  • A Facility is a physical location to which Students and Teachers belong.
  • Organization
  • An Organization is a group of people responsible for administering a set of Zones and Facilities.
  • suggested
  • A Zone is a collection of Devices and Facilities within which student data is synchronized. Two Devices in the same Zone, if synced, can be used interchangeably by students.
